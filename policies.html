System Requirements

Throughout the semester, you will be doing extensive coding in Python. It is your responsibility to ensure that you have the necessary hardware to do this.

  • Computer & Internet: Reliable connection meeting TCU Online requirements
  • TCU Online: Required for this class
  • Google Account: Needed for Drive and Colab
  • TCU Email: Only official student email for notifications

Generative AI Policy

Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology (Google Gemini, ChatGPT, etc.) is permitted and encouraged for your use in this class, provided that you document in detail any and all uses of the technology through descriptive notes (# comments in your code).

Note: Inappropriate or unauthorized use of AI-generated content may be academic misconduct and will be handled according to TCU's Academic Conduct Policy.

APPROACH: Self-Reflection Essays

Method: Three self-reflection essays (about a page each) on your development as a learner
