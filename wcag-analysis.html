Color Contrast Analysis

Current Issues Identified:

  • Links in body text: #c9a9ff on dark backgrounds may not meet WCAG AA contrast requirements (4.5:1)
  • Secondary text: #b19cd9 likely falls below recommended contrast ratios
  • Navigation buttons: Low opacity backgrounds with colored text create accessibility barriers

Recommended Improvements:

  • Use lighter link colors: #e0c4ff or #f0e6ff for better contrast
  • Add underlines to links: Helps users who may have color vision differences
  • Increase contrast for secondary text: Use #d0b3ff instead of #b19cd9
  • Improve button contrast: Use higher opacity backgrounds or lighter text colors
